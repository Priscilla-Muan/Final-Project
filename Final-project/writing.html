Course Overview

Unlock your inner author with our Creative Writing Workshop, ideal for budding writers aged 15 and above. This course guides you through the art of crafting compelling stories, poems, and essays. Through interactive lessons and constructive feedback, you'll learn narrative techniques, character development, and editing skills. Whether you aspire to publish a book or just want to improve your writing, this workshop will provide the tools you need to express yourself creatively and effectively

Course Structure

  • Narrative Spellcasting: Learn to weave compelling plots like a seasoned sorcerer. Our narrative techniques will teach you how to cast spells that captivate readers from the first sentence to the last word.
  • Character Conjuring: Delve deep into the psyche of unforgettable characters. Develop the skills to breathe life into protagonists, villains, and the most whimsical beings, ensuring they step off the page and into the hearts of your audience.
  • Potion of Perspectives: Mix and master the voices of various narrators. Explore first-person sincerity, third-person omniscience, and the mystery of unreliable narrators to find the perfect perspective for your story.
  • Enchanted Editing: Sharpen your quill for the crucial craft of editing. Learn secret incantations to eliminate grammatical gremlins, tighten your narrative, and polish your prose until it shines.
  • Realm of Revisions: Enter the feedback loop where your draft is not just read, but revived. Receive constructive critiques from peers and mentors alike, each aimed at fortifying your manuscript’s magic.
  • Library of Legends: Gain exclusive access to our digital library stocked with works of literary luminaries, mystical writing prompts, and rare manuscripts, providing endless inspiration.


    Special Features:

  • Wizard Workshops: Weekly live sessions with renowned authors and scribes from various realms of the fantasy universe.
  • Dragon’s Den Debates: Engage in fiery discussions about literary theories, the future of storytelling, and the ethics of character fate manipulation.
  • Mystic Mentorship: Pair up with a mentor from our guild of professional writers, poets, and scholars who will guide your journey from novice to novelist.
